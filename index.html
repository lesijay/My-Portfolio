Why I applied to Decagon

After I completed my course with Udacity, I desired a face to face learning environment, where I could further develop my skills to help me launch a career in tech.
I thought to apply for an internship in a tech company, but honestly I did not feel competent enough. When I saw the decagon application advert on linkedIn, I quickly read through their website and I saw a couple of things that caught my attention:

  • "Our curriculum was developed under the guidance of lecturers and industry leaders from Oxford, Stanford and Google, who have had real world experience and have worked on various sucessful tech projects."
  • "At the end of the program you become a competitive entry level Software Developer"
  • "In addition to equipping you with the knowledge and skills you need, we will also place you in a globally competitive job. Paired with one of our top-notch local or international hiring partners, you will earn a middle-class salary."
  • "With our Learn-Then-Earn payment credit, you pay back the cost of the program plus a modest premium once you start working."
Then I thought in my head "What am I waiting for?", I quickly hit the apply button and commited to following through the application processes. I am so excited I did.
